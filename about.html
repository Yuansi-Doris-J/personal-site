// RESEARCH PROJECTS

My research focuses on U.S. Industrial Policy and Fiscal Analysis. As a Research Assistant, I analyzed federal datasets from 1960 to 2024 using Python libraries like Pandas, NumPy, and SciPy. I developed visualization suites using Matplotlib to track structural shifts in policy design and benchmarked U.S. intervention levels against international standards. This work allows me to link policy intensity to sectoral value-added growth, providing a quantitative perspective on long-term economic shifts.
